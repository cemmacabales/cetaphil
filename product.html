How It Works

Advanced Cleansing Technology

Cetaphil's gentle cleansing agents work to remove dirt, oil, and makeup without disrupting the skin's natural moisture barrier. The soap-free formula rinses clean without leaving any residue.

  • Removes 99% of dirt and impurities
  • Preserves natural skin oils
  • Suitable for face and body
  • Can be used with or without water

Usage Instructions

With Water

  1. Apply to wet skin
  2. Gently massage in circular motions
  3. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water
  4. Pat dry with a clean towel

Without Water

  1. Apply to dry skin
  2. Gently massage all over
  3. Remove with soft cloth or tissue
  4. No rinsing required
