About Cetaphil

Over 70 years of dermatological expertise and innovation. Discover the science and research behind gentle, effective skincare.

RESEARCH

Discover the gentle yet effective formulation of Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser, meticulously crafted with a blend of mild surfactants and humectants, including sodium cocoyl isethionate, cocamidopropyl betaine, glycerin, panthenol, and niacinamide. This unique composition ensures the thorough removal of dirt and sebum while actively supporting your skin's hydration and barrier protection. Unlike conventional soap-based cleansers, its non-foaming, lotion-like texture sets it apart, making it exceptionally tolerable for even the most dermatologically vulnerable skin types.

EVALUATION

Backed by rigorous clinical trials, Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser has proven its efficacy in managing skin conditions like rosacea. A clinical study evaluating its effects on patients with moderate rosacea demonstrated that Cetaphil effectively preserves the integrity of the stratum corneum, a crucial factor for maintaining healthy skin. Participants experienced a significant reduction in rosacea severity, with quantifiable improvements noted by dermatologists. The cleanser's humectant properties, derived from ingredients like glycerin and panthenol, ensure optimal hydration and moisture levels. Free from harsh dermatological adversities such as erythema, burning, or xerosis, Cetaphil stands out as a reliable choice for those with highly sensitive and inflamed skin.

DERMATOLOGIST RECOMMENDATION

Esteemed dermatologists highly recommend Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser for its gentle yet effective properties. Its mild formulation makes it suitable for use 2-3 times daily, even for those with sensitive and irritated skin. Particularly beneficial for individuals using retinol, Cetaphil supports the skin's repair barrier, ensuring a balanced and healthy complexion. While not a perfect solution for all skin concerns, its affordability and availability make it a practical choice for many. Dermatologists advise consulting a professional to tailor skincare to individual needs, recognizing that Cetaphil offers a solid foundation for a gentle and effective skincare routine.
