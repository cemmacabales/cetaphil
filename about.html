OUR STORY

A Legacy of Gentle Care

Since 1947, Cetaphil has been pioneering gentle skincare solutions. What started as a single cleanser formulated by a pharmacist has grown into a comprehensive range of products trusted by dermatologists and families worldwide.

Our commitment to gentle, effective skincare has never wavered. Every product is developed with sensitive skin in mind, ensuring that even the most delicate skin types can benefit from our formulations.

Today, Cetaphil continues to innovate while staying true to our founding principles: gentle care, proven results, and dermatologist trust.

COMPLETE INGREDIENT BREAKDOWN

WATER (AQUA)

The base of our formula, providing the foundation for effective delivery of active ingredients while maintaining the product's gentle consistency.

NIACINAMIDE

Also known as Vitamin B3, this ingredient helps reduce hyperpigmentation, promotes even skin tone, and supports the skin's natural barrier function.

PANTHENOL

Pro-Vitamin B5 that helps repair the skin's natural barrier, soothes inflammation, and provides deep moisturization for sensitive skin.

GLYCERIN

A powerful humectant that draws moisture from the environment into the skin, helping maintain hydration and softness throughout the day.

CETEARYL ALCOHOL

A fatty alcohol that acts as an emollient, softening and conditioning the skin without causing dryness or irritation.

PANTOLACTONE

Works synergistically with panthenol to enhance moisture retention and improve skin texture and smoothness.

XANTHAN GUM

A natural thickening agent that provides the cleanser's pleasant gel-like consistency and improves spreadability for easy application.

SODIUM COCOYL ISETHIONATE

A mild, coconut-derived cleansing agent that effectively removes dirt and impurities while being gentle on the skin's natural moisture barrier.

CITRIC ACID

Provides gentle exfoliation by helping to remove dead skin cells, which improves skin clarity, tone, and overall texture.

SODIUM BENZOATE

A safe, effective preservative that ensures the product remains fresh and maintains its efficacy throughout its shelf life.
